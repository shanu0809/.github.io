E-Legal Aid And Welfare Portal

A Web based portal,which focuses on overall development of people by providing a platform to enhance skills, get knowledge of fundamental rights, government schemes and jobs.

Project Overview

This portal is designed as a comprehensive platform to empower individuals, especially in rural and semi-urban areas, by providing critical information and services through a user-friendly interface. It focuses on four main pillars:

The key features of the portal include:

  1. Awareness of Fundamental and Legal Rights:
    The portal offers static but essential information about fundamental rights and legal rights, enabling users to better understand their entitlements and the legal framework protecting them. This knowledge helps foster a more informed and empowered community.

  2. Local Employment Opportunities:
    To curb the trend of migration to larger cities, the portal provides up-to-date information about job openings available within local areas. By connecting job seekers with nearby employment opportunities, it promotes local economic growth and helps individuals sustain themselves without the need to relocate.

  3. Vocational Training and Skill Development:
    Users can explore various vocational training courses available online or search for nearby training centers through the portal. Additionally, they can register for courses directly through the platform, helping them upskill and enhance their employability in today's competitive market.

  4. Government Schemes and Assistance Programs:
    The platform disseminates detailed information about various government welfare schemes and initiatives aimed at supporting different segments of society. This ensures that eligible users can easily learn about and avail themselves of these schemes to improve their livelihood and overall quality of life.

Additional Features:


  • Grievance Redressal:
    Users can submit their grievances directly through the portal, ensuring their concerns reach the appropriate authorities in a structured manner.
  • Community Engagement via Blogs: The portal allows users to write and share blogs on issues related to their rights, local development, and personal experiences, fostering community engagement and dialogue.
